FUTUREHAT — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026 · Developed by Lakshmeshwar Pandey

FUTUREHAT ("the app") is a messaging and calling application. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and your choices. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — the email address and password you use to register, and your display name and optional profile photo. Authentication is handled by Supabase.
  • Messages and media — the text messages, photos, voice notes, and files you send and receive are stored so we can deliver them to your conversations.
  • Status and profile content — statuses you post and profile details you set.
  • Device / push token — a push-notification token so we can deliver message and call notifications to your device.
  • Diagnostics — basic technical logs used to keep calling and sync working reliably.

2. Device permissions

  • Camera — to take and send photos and for video calls.
  • Microphone — to record voice messages and for voice/video calls.
  • Photos / media — to let you attach images and set a profile picture.
  • Biometrics (Face ID / fingerprint) — used only on your device to unlock the app when App Lock is enabled. Biometric data never leaves your device and is never sent to us.
  • Notifications — to alert you to new messages and incoming calls.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide messaging, calling, status, and profile features.
  • To deliver notifications for messages and calls.
  • To secure your account and operate the service reliably.
  • If you purchase FUTUREHAT+, to process payment through our payment provider.

4. Calls

Voice and video calls are established peer-to-peer using WebRTC. Media is encrypted in transit (DTLS-SRTP). Relay (TURN) servers may carry call media when a direct connection is not possible; they route encrypted media and do not store call content.

5. Payments

FUTUREHAT+ subscriptions are processed by Razorpay. Payment card details are handled directly by Razorpay under their own privacy policy; we do not receive or store your full card details.

6. Data sharing

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app — Supabase (database, authentication, storage) and Razorpay (payments) — and only as required to provide the service. We do not sell your personal data or share it for advertising.

7. Data retention and deletion

Your content is retained while your account is active. You can delete individual messages in the app. To delete your account and associated data, use the in-app account deletion option or contact us at the email below; we will remove your personal data except where we are legally required to retain it.

8. Children

FUTUREHAT is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children under that age.

9. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS) and access is protected by authentication and row-level security. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
