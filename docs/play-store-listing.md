## Full description

Paste into Play Console “Full description” (max 4000 characters).

```
Lumixo is chat that keeps friendships alive.

Message people you care about. When you both show up, your streak grows — a simple daily habit that makes staying close feel natural, not forced.

WHY LUMIXO
• Friendship streaks — emoji and score on your chats, details, and shareable moments
• Communities — groups that feel like real clubs, not just folders of chats
• Privacy when you need it — tools like ghost mode and chat lock (where available)
• Fast, familiar messaging — chats, media, voice, stickers, and calls built for daily use
• Lumi — a bit of personality in a world of cold chat apps

STREAKS
Message a friend every day. When you both qualify, your streak climbs through tiers — from first days all the way to Hall of Legends. See streaks on your chat list and in the conversation header. Share your streak and celebrate showing up for each other.

COMMUNITIES
Bring related groups together. Announcements, channels, and a clearer home for the people and clubs that matter.

PRIVATE WHEN YOU NEED IT
Control who sees what. Lock sensitive chats. Use premium privacy features when you want more power — without giving up the joy of staying connected.

BUILT FOR REAL LIFE
• Direct chats and groups
• Photos, videos, voice notes, stickers
• Voice and video calls
• Status updates
• Invite friends with a simple link

Lumixo is for people who want more than another messenger — they want friendships that last.

Download Lumixo. Start a chat. Keep a streak.

Questions? supportlumixo@gmail.com
```

- Use real first names of demo friends (not “User 1”).

- Avoid debug banners, battery %, or personal phone numbers.
